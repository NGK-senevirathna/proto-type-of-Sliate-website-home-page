About SLIATE

Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE) is the premier institution for Higher National Diploma (HND) education in Sri Lanka. Established to provide quality technological education, SLIATE offers career-oriented programs that meet industry demands.

Vision: To be the center of excellence in technological education and training in Sri Lanka.

Mission: To produce competent technologists through quality education and training to meet national and global challenges.

SLIATE operates through a network of Advanced Technological Institutes (ATIs) across Sri Lanka, making quality education accessible to students from all regions.

Academic Programs at SLIATE

SLIATE offers three-year Higher National Diploma (HND) programs in various technological fields. These programs are designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

📱 Information & Communication Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: GCE A/L Technology Stream

Careers: Software Developer, Network Administrator, System Analyst, Database Administrator

⚙️ Engineering Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: GCE A/L Physical Science Stream

Specializations: Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics

📊 Business Management

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: GCE A/L Commerce/Arts Stream

Careers: Business Administrator, Marketing Executive, HR Manager

📚 English

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: GCE A/L Any Stream with good English

Careers: Teacher, Translator, Content Writer, Customer Service

💰 Accounting

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: GCE A/L Commerce Stream

Careers: Accountant, Auditor, Financial Analyst, Tax Consultant

🌱 Agriculture & Food Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: GCE A/L Biological Science Stream

Careers: Agricultural Officer, Food Technologist, Quality Controller

Admission Requirements

To apply for HND programs at SLIATE, students must meet the following minimum requirements:

Basic Requirements for All Programs:

  • Successfully completed GCE Advanced Level Examination
  • Minimum of 3 passes in A/L including English
  • Good pass in English at O/L or A/L
  • Age below 25 years at the time of application

Stream-wise Requirements:

Program Stream Required A/L Subjects Additional Requirements
Engineering Technology Combined Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry Minimum 2 'S' passes or better
Information Technology Any 3 subjects from Technology/Commerce/Arts Credit pass in Mathematics at O/L
Business Management Commerce/Arts subjects Good communication skills
English Any 3 subjects Credit pass in English at A/L preferred
Agriculture Biology, Chemistry, Physics/Agriculture Interest in agriculture sector

Program Duration & Structure

All Higher National Diploma programs at SLIATE follow a structured 3-year curriculum:

Year 1: Foundation

  • Basic theory courses
  • Fundamental practical training
  • Language & communication skills
  • Introduction to specialization

Year 2: Specialization

  • Core subject modules
  • Advanced practical work
  • Project work begins
  • Industry exposure

Year 3: Advanced & Industrial Training

  • Final year project
  • Industrial placement (6 months)
  • Professional development
  • Career preparation

Key Features:

  • 6-month compulsory industrial training in final year
  • Project-based learning approach
  • Modern laboratories and facilities
  • Experienced academic staff
  • Industry-recognized certification
