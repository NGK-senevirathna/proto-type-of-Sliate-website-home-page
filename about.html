About SLIATE

Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education

SLIATE is the main institute for Higher National Diploma (HND) education in Sri Lanka. We provide 3-year diploma programs in technology and business fields.

What We Offer

  • Information Technology programs
  • Engineering Technology courses
  • Business Management studies
  • English Language programs
  • Accounting and Finance

Why Choose SLIATE?

  • Quality education at affordable cost
  • Practical skills for jobs
  • Experienced teachers
  • Modern facilities
  • Campuses across Sri Lanka
